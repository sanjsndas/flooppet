The FloopPet experience

At FloopPet, we believe that every pet deserves a spa day that’s as unique as their breed and personality. Our Full Bath & Breed‑Specific Haircuts service isn’t just about cleaning — it’s a complete wellness ritual. From the moment your furry friend walks through our doors, they are greeted with calming scents, gentle handling, and expertise that comes from over a decade of grooming excellence.

Our signature full bath includes a triple‑rinse system with hypoallergenic, pH‑balanced shampoos and conditioners tailored to coat type. Breed‑specific haircut mastery

Each breed has a standard, but we go beyond — we call it “Floop style”. For Poodles, we offer the continental, modern, or teddy bear cut, always keeping the topknot and pompons crisp. For Schnauzers, we accentuate the beard and eyebrows while maintaining a neat skirt. Terriers get a hand‑stripped look that preserves the wiry texture, while double‑coated breeds like Huskies receive an undercoat blowout that reduces shedding by up to 80%.

Our breed‑specific haircuts are not just about aesthetics; they improve your pet's comfort. We clip sanitary areas, trim paw pads for better traction, and shape the face to enhance vision and eating. We also offer creative styling (dyes, stencils, and glitter) for special occasions — all with pet‑safe products.

We use stainless steel blades that are sterilized after each use, and our drying process uses variable temperature to avoid heat stress. We also integrate calming music and pheromone sprays to make the experience peaceful. Our consultation before every session ensures that we understand your preferences and any special needs (age, health, anxiety).

The full bath ritual

Our full bath is a multi‑step journey: (1) gentle brushing to remove loose fur, (2) a warm water rinse with our proprietary oatmeal or chamomile shampoo, (3) a deep conditioning mask that penetrates the coat, (4) a second rinse with cool water to seal moisture, and (5) a final spritz of argan oil for shine. We also offer a “Pawdicure” package that includes nail filing, pad moisturizing, and a light massage.

We use only eco‑friendly, biodegradable products that are free from parabens, sulfates, and artificial dyes. Our water‑saving system recycles rinse water for our on‑site garden. We also provide a “calm zone” for anxious pets with soft lighting and weighted blankets. After the bath, your pet is fluffed and dried with a high‑velocity dryer that is adjusted to their tolerance.

We are proud to be a plastic‑free salon, using bamboo brushes and compostable aprons. Every full bath includes a complimentary bandana or bow, and we send you a digital “after‑glow” photo. We also offer a loyalty program — every 5th bath is on us! We also partner with local shelters, donating a portion of each bath to pet rescue.
