Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?
While we welcome walk-ins, we highly recommend booking an appointment to ensure availability and minimize wait times. You can book online, by phone, or in person.

What should I bring to my pet's appointment?
Please bring your pet's vaccination records (if not already on file) and any special instructions regarding their health or behavior. We also recommend bringing their favorite treats for positive reinforcement.

How long does a grooming session typically take?
Most Full Bath & Breed‑Specific Haircut sessions take between 90 to 120 minutes, depending on your pet's size, coat condition, and the complexity of the style. We will provide a time estimate during booking.

What if my pet has special needs or anxiety?
We are experienced in handling anxious or special-needs pets. Please inform us in advance so we can prepare a calming environment and tailor our approach. We offer quiet rooms, pheromone sprays, and gentle handling techniques.

Can I stay with my pet during the grooming?
For safety and to minimize distractions, we generally do not allow owners in the grooming area. However, you are welcome to wait in our comfortable reception area, and we will keep you updated on your pet's progress.

Do you offer services for cats?
Yes! We offer gentle grooming for cats, including baths, brushing, and sanitary trims. Please note that cat grooming requires a separate booking and may take additional time.

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, and mobile payment services (Apple Pay, Google Pay). We do not accept checks.
