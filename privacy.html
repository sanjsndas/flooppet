Privacy Policy

Your data, our responsibility · FloopPet

Introduction

FloopPet (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, book services, or interact with us in person. We adhere to the principles of data minimization, transparency, and user control.

This policy applies to all users, clients, and visitors. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We may update this page from time to time, and we encourage you to review it periodically. The latest version is always available on our website.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Identification: Name, email address, phone number, postal address, pet’s name, breed, age, and health notes (allergies, medications).
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, billing address, and transaction history (processed securely through our payment partners).
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, referral source, and other diagnostic data when you interact with our website.
  • Pet Photos & Grooming Records: Images taken during grooming sessions (with your consent) and notes on coat condition, behavior, and preferences.
  • Communication Data: Records of emails, chat messages, phone calls, and feedback you provide.

We do not collect sensitive data (e.g., racial origin, political opinions, health data beyond pet health) unless you voluntarily provide it for service adaptation. All data is collected lawfully and with your explicit consent.

How We Use Your Information

FloopPet uses your data for the following purposes:

  • To process bookings, appointments, and payments.
  • To personalize grooming sessions (breed-specific haircuts, product selection).
  • To communicate with you about appointments, reminders, and follow-ups.
  • To send promotional offers, newsletters, and event invitations (you may opt out at any time).
  • To improve our services, website functionality, and customer support.
  • To maintain records for legal and regulatory compliance.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, security incidents, or abuse.

We never sell your data to third parties. We may share data with trusted partners (e.g., payment processors, IT providers) only as necessary to operate our business, and they are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity: To fulfill our service agreement with you (e.g., booking a grooming session).
  • Legitimate interests: To improve our services, send appointment reminders, and for internal analytics.
  • Consent: For marketing communications, photo usage, and optional data collection (you can withdraw consent anytime).
  • Legal obligations: To comply with tax, accounting, and health & safety regulations.

We always balance our legitimate interests against your privacy rights and ensure that processing is necessary and proportionate.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Generally:

  • Client records (name, contact, pet details) are kept for 7 years after your last interaction for legal and tax purposes.
  • Marketing data is retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • Grooming notes and photos are kept for 3 years to ensure continuity of care (you may request deletion earlier).
  • Payment data is retained only as long as required by our payment processors and financial regulations.

After the retention period, data is securely anonymized or deleted.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Access controls: only authorized personnel have access to personal data, and they are trained in privacy practices.
  • Regular security audits, vulnerability scanning, and penetration testing.
  • Secure storage of physical records in locked cabinets with limited access.
  • Incident response plan in case of a data breach (we will notify affected users and authorities as required by law).

While no system is 100% secure, we continuously update our protocols to safeguard your information.

Third-Party Sharing

We may share your data with the following categories of third parties:

  • Service providers: Payment gateways (e.g., Stripe, PayPal), cloud hosting (AWS), email delivery platforms, and scheduling software. They process data only on our behalf and cannot use it for their own purposes.
  • Legal authorities: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of FloopPet, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred as part of the transaction, with prior notice.

We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers. All third-party processors are vetted for compliance with GDPR/CCPA standards.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Restriction: Limit processing of your data under certain circumstances.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
  • Withdraw consent: At any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@floopet.com or call +1 (800) 555‑FLOOP. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. For more details, please see our Cookies Policy.

We use both session cookies (temporary) and persistent cookies (for preferences and analytics). We do not use tracking cookies for cross-site advertising.

Children’s Privacy

FloopPet services are intended for pet owners, who are generally adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians may contact us to request removal.

International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States, but we may use cloud services that store data in other regions. When we transfer data across borders, we ensure appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable laws.

Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on our website. The “Last Updated” date at the bottom of this page indicates the latest revision. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Complaints and Supervisory Authority

If you have concerns about our data handling, please contact us first. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g., ICO in the UK, CCPA in California, or the relevant authority in your region). We will cooperate fully with regulatory bodies to resolve any issues.

Additional Information

Data Collection Methods: We collect data directly from you (when you fill forms, book online, or visit our salon), automatically via our website (cookies, logs), and from third-party referral partners (only if you provide consent). We also collect data from social media interactions if you engage with our pages (e.g., Facebook, Instagram).

Anonymized Data: We may use aggregated, anonymized data for research, analytics, and improving our services. This data does not identify you and is not subject to this policy.

Do Not Track Signals: Our website does not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals, but we offer cookie controls and opt-out options for targeted advertising.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA): California residents have the right to request disclosure of data collected, deletion, and opt-out of sale (we do not sell data). To exercise these rights, contact us at the details above.

GDPR (EU/UK): If you are in the EEA or UK, you have additional rights under GDPR. Our Data Protection Officer can be contacted at dpo@floopet.com. We process data based on the lawful bases described above.

Data Breach Notification: In the unlikely event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours, as required by law.

Third-Party Links: Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., social media, partner services). We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their policies.

Automated Decision-Making: We do not use automated decision-making (e.g., profiling) that significantly affects you. All appointments and grooming plans are reviewed by our trained staff.

Retention of Pet Data: Pet health and grooming data is retained to ensure consistent care. You may request that we delete your pet’s data at any time, except when we are legally required to keep it (e.g., for liability or tax purposes).

Marketing Preferences: You can opt out of marketing emails by clicking the “unsubscribe” link in any email or by contacting us. We will honour your preference within 10 business days.

Data Portability: We can provide a copy of your data in CSV or JSON format upon request. We will provide this free of charge, but we may charge a reasonable fee for repetitive or excessive requests.

Security Training: All FloopPet employees undergo annual privacy and security training. We also conduct regular internal audits to ensure compliance with this policy.

Incident Response: We have a detailed incident response plan that includes containment, investigation, notification, and remediation. We also maintain cyber insurance to cover potential damages.

Consent Withdrawal: If you withdraw consent for marketing or optional data collection, we will stop using your data for those purposes, but we may continue to process data necessary for service delivery or legal compliance.

Changes to Contact Details: If you change your email or phone number, please update your profile or notify us so that we can keep your records accurate.

Data Subject Requests: We strive to respond to all valid requests within 30 days. If we cannot comply (e.g., due to legal exemptions), we will explain the reasons in writing.

Feedback: We welcome your feedback on this Privacy Policy. Please send your comments to privacy@floopet.com.
